Who We Are

We are Algo5, a team of five innovators passionate about empowering young people through civic education.

Our Mission & Vision

HerRights is dedicated to empowering young women (18-24) through accessible civic education. We believe that when women understand their rights and how to exercise them, they become powerful agents of change in their communities.

Our vision is a world where all young women are equipped with the knowledge, tools, and confidence to participate fully in civic life and shape the policies that affect them.

Empowerment

We provide tools and knowledge that enable young women to claim their rights and spaces.

Accessibility

We believe civic education should be available to everyone, regardless of background.

Innovation

We use technology to create engaging, modern solutions to age-old problems.

Collaboration

We work with communities, organizations, and young women themselves to create change.
